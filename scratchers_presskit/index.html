Scratchers is a pixel art incremental game where you scratch cards to earn cash, unlock upgrades, and hire little scratchers to do the scratching for you. Open booster packs filled with new and exotic scratch card types each with unique mechanics, themes, and rarities. Hire little scratchers and climb the economic ladder as you go from casual scratcher to corporate magnate. Your ultimate goal? Amass a fortune so vast that you can retire as the richest person to ever exist!

Features:

  • Scratch cards to earn cash, collect, and buy better cards
  • Diverse set of scratch cards, each with unique designs
  • Hire scratcher to help you!
  • Satisfying and cozy pixel art design
  • Strategic progression experience. Prestige system, reset your progress, but be stronger than ever!
  • A set of custom booster pack to unlcok new powerful cards!
